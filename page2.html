Travel


"We travel, some of us forever, to seek other states, other lives, other souls."
--Anais Nin

I have not had the opportunity to travel anywhere near as much (or as far!) as I would like. But wanderlust has taken me to some pretty amazing places!

My favorite so far is Banff Springs and Lake Louise in Alberta, Canada. Without a doubt, it is the most beautiful, magical, place I have ever been.

Other places I've loved include Portland, OR, Wolf Creek, CO, Venice Beach, CA, and Cabo San Lucas in Mexico.
